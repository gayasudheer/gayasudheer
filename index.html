About Me

My name is Gayathri (Gaya) Sudheer and I am a senior studying CIS. My dream job is to become a data engineer in the healthcare industry. Originally, I wanted to go into the healthcare field and become a PA or doctor but decided against it after shadowing some professionals who worked in the industry. I've always had a strong interest in data and the healthcare field so a data engineering was the perfect combination for me. For this role, having a strong foundation in databases (both structured and unstructured), data warehousing, data architecture, and various coding languages like SQL and Python are necessary to be successful. Data engineers also need to have solid statistics knowledge, analytical skills, and familiarity with Big Data technologies. They are responsible for designing, constructing, testing, and maintaining databases of all sizes. Aside from technical skills, they also need strong communication, collaboration, and presentation skills especially when working in teams and explaining information to non-technical people.

Ideally, I would like to work in a in-house environment. I also like working on teams and working alongside my coworkers so working in-house seems like a great option. While freelancing sounds very appealing, the benefits of an in-house environment offers more stability and benefits like a 401K and healthcare plans that outweigh the benefits of freelancing. The healthcare industry is also less prone to the impacts of the fluctuating economy so there will be more stability with the role as well. Eventually after working in the industry for a couple years gaining experience, I would like to try out consulting.
